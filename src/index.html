The First Vision

In the spring of 1820, God the Father and Jesus Christ appeared to Joseph Smith as he prayed in a grove of trees near his home in western New York. This event is known as the First Vision.

A Note from the Author

What is this website for and why does it exist?

What is The First Vision .Org?

The First Vision .Org answers simple questions

What

The First Vision .Org aims to answer simple questions about Joseph Smith's First Vision. It answers questions such as, "what is the first vision?", "why is the first vision important?", and many others.

Why Does The First Vision .Org Exist?

A brief explanation as to why

Why

The website initially started as a school project. I soon realized that there was a lot of information about Joseph Smith's First Vision, however it was scattered and difficult to find it all. I wanted to create a website where a lot of the information is easily accessible and available for those with questions to find answers in the information. Ultimately, some of the answers will not be able to be answered by documents, videos, or other sources of information. A person must seek answers from God through prayer just as Joseph Smith did (see James 1:5 ).

From the Author

A Personal Note from the Author

It is important to determine for yourself if Joseph Smith's First vision actually happened or not. It is nice to know about, but determining for yourself if it is real or not is life changing. Please do as James suggests and ask of God if it happened through prayer. If you are unsure of how to ask or pray please learn how by reading this page .
